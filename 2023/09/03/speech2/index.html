My Dream

–A speech of RB

Ladies and gentlemen:

Hello. I’m Fashion Zhi. Thank you to come to me. l have a speech about dream.

Many people have their own dream. Someone’s dream is about being rich. Someone’s dream is travelling to Britain. And more people dream to be a doctor, a worker, a teacher and so on. But my dream is special: l have a dream, that is I can plant the seeds of grapes all around the world.

Maybe it’s difficult, but I will reach my dream diligently. When I was three years old, I ate my first grape, gently peeling the skin of the grape, and a tender flesh appeared in front of your eyes. The flesh is translucent and looks like crystal, like agate. Exudes a charming fragrance, makes the population water straight. Can not wait to put into the mouth, fine chew, sweet and delicious juice suddenly permeated the whole body, the sweet taste has always remained in my mouth, a long time will not disappear. I fell in love with the taste of grapes, So l have dreamt to grow the seeds of grapes when I was three. When I was six, I watched my first movie about growing grapes. I was learnt a lot of knowledge of planting grape seeds from it. After that, I have watched thousands of movie about growing grapes. A year ago, I think it was the best time for me to plant seeds of grapes for the first time. I bought a lot of grape seeds. A sunny morning, I have embarked on the journey of growing grapes.

The road to your dream isn’t always easy. I was so excited to start growing grapes, but it turned out to be a failure. I followed all the instructions carefully, provided enough water and sunlight, but still, the plants didn’t grow well, some even died. I felt really disappointed and didn’t know what went wrong. Later, found out that I needed to add some fertilizer to provide the necessary nutrients for the plants to grow. I learned a valuable lesson and now know better for my next attempt.

I began a second attempt. With careful research and preparation, I has chosen a suitable location that provides optimal conditions for grape cultivation. Spending hours each day, I tends to my grapevines with love and attention, ensuring they receive the necessary sunlight, water, and nutrients. I prunes and trains the vines meticulously to encourage healthy growth and abundant fruit production. I diligently monitors the soil quality and pH levels, making necessary adjustments to maintain an ideal environment for my grapes. As the seasons progress, my hard work pays off when I finally reaps the rewards of his labor-a bountiful harvest of delicious and juicy grapes. I’m glad I’m one step closer to my dream.

Dreams can’t be realized so smoothly, On the way to realizing the dream, there are countless more problems that come along with it. When I made this plan public, A lot of people are against me. They don’t think grapes are fit to be grown all over the world. They came to laugh at me, insult me, denigrate me, and even try to kill me. My vineyards are ruined by them. Confusion and loss plagued me, causing me to fall towards the abyss without realizing it. Until one stormy night, I came across Xiao Qiang’s story that encouraged me.

Xiao Qiang’s family is not rich and his parents want him to choose a stable job, but Xiao Qiang insists on his dream of music. He works part-time during the day and devotes a lot of time at night to create music with a unique style, full of passion and infectious energy. However, his work is not recognized in competitions and the judges are confused about his music. This makes Xiao Qiang feel lost and confused. In the pursuit of his dream, Xiao Qiang also suffered financial difficulties and his father fell ill, but while he worked harder every day, he still insisted on his dream. He attended music training courses and sought advice from famous teachers to improve his musical skills. His music gradually gained attention. A famous music producer discovered Xiao Qiang’s work, praised his music and invited him to collaborate on an album. The album brought Xiao Qiang’s name to the forefront of the music industry and people began to recognize his talent.

I reflected on myself, wasn’t I in the same predicament as Xiao Qiang before me? Why don’t stick to myself like he did?

I pulled myself together. I created an R-team of supportive people and me. We fought against those who opposed us. Although we always fail, our will is rock solid and we are never afraid of failure. Under my leadership, l believe that the R-team will always persevere and will eventually win!

Though many people don’t agree with me yet, I still try to grow grapes. It’s still my dream. All the time, l remember a sentence: I have a dream, that is I can plant the seeds of grapes all around the world!
